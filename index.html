Our Chefs

Coming right from Japan to bring the best on your plate

Yuki Nakamura
Chef de Cuisine

Born in a family of well known chefs from Tokyo, Yuki Nakamura has been inspired by his mother ever since he was a child. After mastering the art of Japanese cuisine, he made sure to bring it outside of his country as well. 

Kenji Yatake
Sous Chef

His restaurants are one of the most popular around Japan. Tourists from all over the world make sure to stop by and try his renowned dishes. And now you can do it too at Daidokoro.

Haru Natsu
Chef de Partie

Many recognise her after winning the last year's edition of Masterchef. She has impressed pretentious chefs and countless people with her cooking. Ever since, she has been one of the most popular chefs of the moment.

The best items on our menu

Shrimp Ramen 

Ramen for everybody


Ramen is one of the most popular Japanese dishes thanks to its various combinations of ingredients that never fail to bring an element of surprise. The broth, the meat, the eggs, the noodles, the unique soy sauce taste and the various toppings will sure bring Japan closer to your heart.

Philadelphia Roll

Sushi, sushi, sushi


Maybe the most famous dish that Japan had to offer to the world. Sushi is never a bad option, as it can be served in such combinations that will please everyone. You have never had sushi? This is your sign to do it! Pick your favorite ingredients and let us work our magic to help you understand what a truly Japanese dish is.

Red Bean Mochi 

Mochi time

You will not regret it.
